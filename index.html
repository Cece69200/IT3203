What is Web Analytics?

Web analytics is the process of collecting, analyzing, and reporting website data to understand and improve online performance. It helps website owners and businesses track visitor behavior, measure traffic sources, and evaluate the effectiveness of their content and marketing efforts.

By using tools like Google Analytics, website administrators can learn what pages people visit, how long they stay, what devices they use, and where they're coming from.

Why is Web Analytics Important?

Understand Your Audience

Track who visits your site and how they interact with your content.

Improve User Experience

Discover which pages work best and where users drop off.

Boost Conversions

Use data to optimize marketing and increase sales or engagement.
