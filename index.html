Understanding Web Analytics

Web analytics is the process of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data related to the behavior of visitors on a website. It helps businesses and individuals make informed decisions to improve user experience, drive more traffic, and boost conversions.

By tracking key metrics such as traffic sources, page views, bounce rate, and conversion rates, organizations can identify what works well and what needs improvement.
