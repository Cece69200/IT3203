Understanding Web Analytics

Web analytics is the practice of collecting, analyzing, and reporting data about website visitors and their interactions.

It helps you understand how users engage with your site, which pages perform well, and where improvements are needed to enhance user experience.

With the right tools and insights, web analytics empowers smarter decisions, improved design, and more effective digital marketing strategies.
