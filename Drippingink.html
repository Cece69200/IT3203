Welcome to our digital gallery exploring the evolution of technology. Discover how innovations from the past have shaped our modern world in computing, communication, and beyond.
1940s - ENIAC: One of the first general-purpose electronic digital computers. Marked the beginning of the computer age.
1980s - Mobile Phones: The Motorola DynaTAC became the first commercial handheld mobile phone, revolutionizing communication.
1990s - The World Wide Web: Tim Berners-Lee introduced the WWW, making the internet more accessible to the public and shaping online communication.
2000s - Smartphones: Apple's iPhone launched in 2007, combining a phone, camera, and internet access in one powerful device.
2020s - Artificial Intelligence: Tools like ChatGPT and AI assistants are transforming the way we interact, learn, and work.
